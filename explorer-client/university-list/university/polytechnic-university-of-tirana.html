Polytechnic University of Tirana

The Polytechnic University of Tirana is a leading public technical university located in Tirana, Albania. It was founded in 1951 and has since then been a pioneer in providing high-quality education in engineering, architecture, and technology-related fields.

Faculties

The Polytechnic University of Tirana offers a wide range of faculties, each dedicated to a specific area of study. Some of the faculties include:

  1. Faculty of Civil Engineering: This faculty offers programs in civil engineering, environmental engineering, and geodesy and geomatics.
  2. Faculty of Electrical Engineering: This faculty offers programs in electrical engineering, electronics, telecommunications, and computer engineering.
  3. Faculty of Mechanical Engineering: This faculty offers programs in mechanical engineering, mechatronics, and automotive engineering.
  4. Faculty of Architecture and Urban Planning: This faculty offers programs in architecture, urban planning, and interior design.
  5. Faculty of Information Technology: This faculty offers programs in computer science, software engineering, and information systems.
  6. Faculty of Mining and Petroleum Engineering: This faculty offers programs in mining engineering, petroleum engineering, and geology.
  7. Faculty of Mathematics and Physics: This faculty offers programs in mathematics, physics, and applied physics.
  8. Faculty of Economics and Management: This faculty offers programs in economics, business administration, and management.
  9. Faculty of Law: This faculty offers programs in law and legal studies.
