University of Bialystok Exchange Program

Within the framework of the Erasmus+ program, a call for applications for scholarships for student mobility has been opened for students from the University of Tirana to the University of Bialystok, Poland, for the first semester of the academic year 2023-2024.

The types of mobility include: Student exchange for studies.

The levels of mobility for students include: Bachelor and Master.

The duration of the scholarships is as follows: Bachelor (1 semester), Master(1 semester).

Fields of study

  • Architectural Design
  • Computer Science
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering and Environmental Sciences
  • Architecture
  • Foreign Language
